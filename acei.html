information

Açaí berries are small, dark purple fruits with a rich, chocolate-like flavor. They are exceptionally high in antioxidants and healthy fats.

Growing Areas

Açaí palms are native to the Amazon rainforest in South America, primarily in Brazil.

Health Benefits

Açaí berries are touted for their potential antioxidant and anti-inflammatory effects.
